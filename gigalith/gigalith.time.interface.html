str->local-date

(str->local-date ds)

Converts ds to a java.time.LocalDate. Supports dates in formats:

  • yyyy-MM-dd
  • yyyy/MM/dd
  • MM-dd-yyyy
  • MM/dd/yyyy

Arg Schemas:

[(ds :- :string)]

Returns:

:time/local-date
