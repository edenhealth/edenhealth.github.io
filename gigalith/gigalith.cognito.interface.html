gigalith.cognito.interface

Cognito brick

Instantiates an AWS CognitoIDP client.

To mount:

(declare cognitoidp)

(mount/defstate cognitoidp
  :start (init! <access-key-id>
                <secret-access-key>
                <region>
                <user-pool-id>
                <client-id or nil>))

If client-id is nil, sign-up will throw. Use create-user instead.
