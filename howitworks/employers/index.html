We make healthcare work for businesses.

Eden Health’s comprehensive approach meaningfully improves health outcomes for employees and reduces the total cost of care to employers.

For common conditions, our doctors and nurses treat patients both in-person and virtually. For more complex conditions requiring a specialist’s expertise, we refer to high-value providers who have a proven track record for high quality and high value.

Save on healthcare costs

Eden Health removes financial and logistical barriers to receiving care. By improving access and convenience for employees, we make it easy for employees to receive the right care at the right time at the right place.

Population Health Management

Surface prevalent conditions within the employee base. We also work with employers to implement high-impact programming that targets the specific issues that your employees are facing.

Curated, value-based referrals

We use industry-leading pricing and quality data to curate our referrals to providers that have a proven track record of high quality. These value-based referrals drive significant cost savings and improve patient satisfaction.
